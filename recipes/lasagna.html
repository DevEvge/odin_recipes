Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna ingridients

  • Meat sauce: 500g
  • Cheese mixtura: 500g
  • Layers: 12 lasagna noodles

Instruction

  1. the Sauce:
    In a large pan, brown the ground beef with onions and garlic.
    Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
    Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Mix Cheeses
    In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, and half of the Parmesan.
  3. Assemble:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
    • Place a layer of noodles on top.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture and sprinkle with mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a thick layer of mozzarella and the remaining Parmesan on top.
  4. Bake:
    Cover with foil and bake at 190°C for 25 minutes.
    Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  5. Rest:
    Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing.
